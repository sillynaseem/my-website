Hi! I'm Naseem.

I live a quiet life. Writing comes easy and books were my first friends. If I were queen, I would mandate morning walks and afternoon naps for all. I thrive on working remotely and moving around freely, which is what I love about freelancing. I take care of myself, sit in the sun, listen to music, stretch out, and in general feel much fondness for the growing movement of digital nomads, of which I consider myself part.

Prior to freelancing, I dedicated myself to academia and nonprofits, earning a Master of Public Health degree and supporting efforts to dismantle mass incarceration and grow greater freedom and power for people living with HIV/AIDS.
